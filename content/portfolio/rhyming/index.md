## Book Summary
Momo, SlowMo and FroMo are having lots of fun, as they indulge themselves in playing a Rhyming Game. Would you also like to join them and see what this game is all about? At the same time, you can also challenge yourself and your friends, to see what other rhyming words you can come up with.
